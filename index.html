Superate y Triunfa

Supérate y Triunfa was a migration from an HTML/PHP based site to Drupal 7. It involved:

  • Services Module to synchronize the user registration through the migration
  • Build course catalog of in-site and external classes
  • Create Virtual/Physical classes, along with syllabuses and grading for each student
  • Mobile version of selected courses

The number of classes has grown exponentially, and has allowed Superate y Triunfa to grow to several cities in the US.

The Avalon Music

http://theavalonmusic.com

The Avalon Music

The Avalon Music provides live music for different events. It had a collection of several hundred music files posted on their Drupal 5 website, which it needed to migrate.

We moved all their information to an interim Drupal 6 Site, which allowed for a smooth transition of all their content to a Drupal 7 site.

This transition allowed us to create a new responsive custom theme for the website, and to migrate to HTML5 music players. This allowed The Avalon Music to move their business to mobile users, key for their continued growth.
